About

I am a second-year PhD student at KIAA, Peking University, advised by Prof. Linhua Jiang. Prior to this, I obtained a B.S. degree in Astronomy from Department of Astronomy, School of Physic, Peking University. My current research focuses on the black hole mass estimator of Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) and the AGN large-scale feedback. I'm also working on the data reduction of James Webb Space Telescope (JWST), especially JWST NIRCam Grism dataset.
